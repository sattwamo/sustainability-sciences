Master of Science in Sustainability Sciences

Admissions Open for Autumn 2025 at IISER Kolkata

Why Sustainability Science?

From clean energy to resilient ecosystems and responsible consumption, sustainability science connects science and society. Our MS programme empowers future leaders to tackle the world's biggest environmental and policy challenges.

Programme Structure

  • 2 Year MS Programme
  • Intake: 15 students/year

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in Chemistry, Environmental Science, Biotechnology, Life Science, Earth Science
  • Final year students may apply
  • Minimum 60% marks or equivalent grade

Qualified in: JAM, GATE, CUET

How to Apply

Step 1: Submit online application
Step 2: Save and print application slip (PDF)
Step 3: Appear for offline interview with:
  • Mark sheets & certificates
  • Identity proof
  • Aptitude test scorecard
